Our story

Cats deserve real food.

So we brought the best of it to Bangladesh.

It started with one very unimpressed cat

Belle turned her nose up at every bag on the shelf. The ingredient lists read like chemistry homework — corn, "meat meal," mystery colours. We figured our cats deserved better than leftovers dressed up in shiny packaging.

So we went and found the food we wished existed — super-premium ranges like Reflex Plus and Prostar, built on real, named proteins with no corn, wheat, soy or artificial anything. Then we brought them to Bangladesh and priced them to make sense in taka, delivered to your door.

What we promise

Real meat is always the first ingredient. If your cat doesn't love it, we refund you — even if the bag is open. Only brands we'd feed our own cats make it onto the shelf. That's the whole deal.
